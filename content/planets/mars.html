♂ Mars

Evergreen notes • Stories • Food pairings

Mythology & Stories

The Roman god of war and action, Mars was known for courage and boldness. In astrology, it sparks ambition and drive. A lively tale says Mars once challenged the Sun to a race… and lost, but claimed he was “just giving him a head start.”

Food & Flavors

  • Spicy curries, chili-infused chocolate.
  • Smoky grilled meats or roasted peppers.
  • Drink pairing: fiery ginger beer or spiced mulled wine.

Entertaining Bits

From “Mars Attacks!” to sports team mascots, Mars is all action. Proverb: “Fortune favors the bold.” Meme-able: “Running on Mars energy: 0 to overly competitive in 3 seconds.”
