Evergreen notes • Stories • Food pairings

Mythology & Stories

The Roman goddess of love and beauty, Venus was said to be born from sea foam. In many cultures, Venus symbolizes harmony, art, and sensual pleasures. A sweet legend says roses first blushed pink when Venus brushed past them in a garden.

Food & Flavors

  • Chocolate-covered strawberries, rose-flavored desserts.
  • Fresh figs and pomegranate seeds.
  • Drink pairing: sparkling rosé with a sprig of lavender.

Entertaining Bits

Botticelli’s “The Birth of Venus” and endless romantic playlists. Proverb: “Where there is love, there is life.” Meme-able: “Venus in my chart = hopeless romantic… with great taste.”
