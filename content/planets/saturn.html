♄ Saturn

Evergreen notes • Stories • Food pairings

Mythology & Stories

Saturn, the Roman god of time and agriculture, is a patient teacher. Known for discipline and long-term rewards, Saturn reminds us that good things take time. An old joke says Saturn invented deadlines just to see if humans could handle them.

Food & Flavors

  • Slow-cooked soups and stews.
  • Dark chocolate, aged cheese, and root vegetables.
  • Drink pairing: a strong cup of black tea.

Entertaining Bits

Featured in Goya’s dramatic painting “Saturn Devouring His Son.” Proverb: “Patience is bitter, but its fruit is sweet.” Meme-able: “Saturn: your cosmic life coach… with tough love.”
