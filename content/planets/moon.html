☾ Moon

Evergreen notes • Stories • Food pairings

Mythology & Stories

In Greek lore, Selene rides her silver chariot through the night sky. In Hindu stories, Chandra is the Moon God who carries nectar of immortality. Folklore says the Moon influences tides, moods, and even hair growth. A charming superstition claims that if you whisper your wish to the first crescent you see, it may just come true.

Food & Flavors

  • Creamy cheeses, moon-shaped dumplings, and soft pastries.
  • White chocolate mousse or coconut-based desserts.
  • Drink pairing: warm chamomile tea with a drizzle of honey.

Entertaining Bits

Featured in countless lullabies and Van Morrison’s “Moondance.” Proverb: “The Moon does not fight; it only unveils beauty.” Meme-able: “Currently in my moon phase… and my blanket burrito phase.”
