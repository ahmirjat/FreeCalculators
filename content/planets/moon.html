Mythology & Stories

Messenger of the gods in Roman mythology, Mercury is quick-witted, sharp-tongued, and always on the move. In astrology, it rules communication and travel. A playful anecdote says Mercury invented the first “express delivery” service — winged sandals included.

Food & Flavors

  • Spicy ginger tea for mental clarity.
  • Drink pairing: an espresso shot to keep up with Mercury’s pace.

Entertaining Bits
