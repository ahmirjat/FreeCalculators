Mythology & Stories

Uranus, the primordial sky god, represents sudden change and innovation. In astrology, it’s the spark of rebellion and originality. Legend has it Uranus gifted lightning to innovators just to “see what they’d do with it.”

Food & Flavors

  • Unusual fusion dishes, electric-blue cocktails.
  • Anything pickled or fermented with a twist.
  • Drink pairing: sparkling kombucha with a splash of lime.

Entertaining Bits
