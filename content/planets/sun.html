☉ Sun

Evergreen notes • Stories • Food pairings

Mythology & Stories

In nearly every culture, the Sun is a divine timekeeper — from Ra’s golden barge sailing across the Egyptian sky to Surya’s radiant chariot in Hindu lore. Ancient Greeks saw it as Helios, a shining guardian driving a fiery chariot each day. One delightful tale says roosters greet the Sun with a song, ensuring it remembers to rise. Without their call, who knows — we might all sleep in!

Food & Flavors

  • Citrus fruits like oranges and lemons — bright, zesty, and full of life.
  • Golden saffron rice or honey-drizzled pastries.
  • Drink pairing: a chilled glass of sparkling lemonade with a hint of mint.

Entertaining Bits

From Van Gogh’s “Sunflowers” to The Beatles’ “Here Comes the Sun,” this star gets plenty of fan mail. Proverb: “Turn your face to the sun and the shadows fall behind you.” Meme-able: “Powered by solar energy… and coffee.”
