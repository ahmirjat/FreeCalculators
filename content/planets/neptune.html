♆ Neptune

Evergreen notes • Stories • Food pairings

Mythology & Stories

Neptune, god of the sea in Roman mythology, rules dreams, illusions, and inspiration. Some say he stirs the oceans when bored, creating waves just for fun. His trident symbolizes both power and mystery.

Food & Flavors

  • Seafood dishes, seaweed snacks, and salted caramel.
  • Ocean-blue desserts and tropical fruits.
  • Drink pairing: a coconut water mojito.

Entertaining Bits

Seen in movies like “Aquaman” and classical paintings. Proverb: “Still waters run deep.” Meme-able: “Neptune vibes: dreamy but slightly lost at sea.”
