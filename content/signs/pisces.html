♓ Pisces

Evergreen notes • Stories • Food pairings

Mythology & Stories

Pisces is the Fishes, linked to the myth of Aphrodite and Eros escaping the monster Typhon by leaping into the sea—either transforming into fish or being carried away by them. The two are bound by a cord so they never lose each other in the deep. Pisces energy is intuitive, compassionate, and dream-woven, swimming between reality and imagination. Fun bit: some old star charts draw them tail-to-tail, tethered like dance partners in a cosmic waltz.

Food & Flavors

  • Vibe: fluid, delicate, and a little ethereal.
  • Herbs & notes: dill, mint, coriander, sea salt, coconut.
  • Produce: cucumber, zucchini, melon, citrus, leafy greens.
  • Mains: seafood dishes, light curries, miso soup, fresh spring rolls.
  • Drink/Dessert: coconut water or lavender lemonade; key lime pie or mango pudding.

Entertaining Bits

Pop-culture nod: the dreamlike visuals of The Shape of Water. Proverb: “A smooth sea never made a skilled sailor.” Meme-able line: “Pisces: living in a daydream (and it’s beautiful here).”
