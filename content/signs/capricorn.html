♑ Capricorn

Evergreen notes • Stories • Food pairings

Mythology & Stories

Capricorn is the Sea‑Goat: ancient images show a mountain‑climbing goat with a fishtail—ambition that rises from deep waters. One tale links it to Pan diving into the Nile and half‑shifting to escape Typhon; another ties it to midwinter rites of Saturn, when patience and planning kept communities alive. Capricorn energy builds brick by brick, aiming for summits that last. Fun bit: the Sun enters Capricorn near the year’s darkest days—perfect symbolism for lantern‑bearers who keep going.

Food & Flavors

  • Vibe: classic, hearty, and earned—substance over flash.
  • Herbs & notes: rosemary, thyme, sage, black pepper, browned butter.
  • Produce: mushrooms, potatoes, parsnips, kale, onions—cold‑weather staples.
  • Mains: slow braises, roast vegetables, barley risotto, herb‑crusted roast.
  • Drink/Dessert: strong black tea or espresso; biscotti or dark‑chocolate bark with nuts.

Entertaining Bits

Pop‑culture nod: the grit and problem‑solving of The Martian. Proverb: “Measure twice, cut once.” Meme‑able line: “Capricorn: calendar has a calendar… and a contingency plan.”
