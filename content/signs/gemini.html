Mythology & Stories

Gemini is the Twins, Castor and Pollux, famed in Greek myth as inseparable brothers—one mortal, one divine. When Castor died, Pollux begged Zeus to let them share immortality; the king of gods placed them side by side in the heavens. Gemini energy is quick, curious, and always moving between worlds. Fun bit: sailors once prayed to the Twins for calm seas— seeing their twin stars rise meant safe travels ahead.
