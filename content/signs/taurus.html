♉ Taurus

Evergreen notes • Stories • Food pairings

Mythology & Stories

Taurus is the celestial Bull, linked to spring’s lush return. In Greek myth, Zeus transformed into a white bull to carry Europa across the sea; other starry neighbors (Hyades and Pleiades) mark the Bull’s face and shoulder. Taurus energy is steady craft and patient pleasure—beauty built to last. Fun bit: ancient farmers watched Taurus rise at dawn as a green‑light to plant, feast, and sing to the fields.

Food & Flavors

  • Vibe: cozy, slow, and sensual—comfort with quality.
  • Herbs & notes: thyme, basil, vanilla, cinnamon, honey, olive oil.
  • Produce: figs, pears, berries, potatoes, mushrooms, cream‑friendly greens.
  • Mains: slow‑roasted veg/meats, creamy risotto, butter‑basted anything.
  • Drink/Dessert: chai latte or cocoa; cheesecake, tiramisu, or honey‑fig tart.

Entertaining Bits

Pop‑culture nod: the sensual food love of Chocolat. Proverb: “Slow and steady wins the race.” Meme‑able line: “Taurus: ‘Treat yourself’ is a lifestyle, not a slogan.”
