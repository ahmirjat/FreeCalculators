Mythology & Stories

Libra is the Scales—symbol of balance and fair dealing. In mythic threads it’s linked to Themis or Dike, keepers of cosmic order, weighing truth with a steady hand. Libra energy seeks equilibrium, elegance, and artful compromise: the right words at the right time. Fun bit: near the autumn equinox, day and night balance—sky poetry that mirrors Libra’s favorite theme.
