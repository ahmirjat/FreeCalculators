Mythology & Stories

Aquarius is the Water Bearer—often Ganymede, the brilliant youth carried to the heavens to pour refreshment for the gods. In other threads it’s a rain‑caller, tipping a jar that renews parched lands. Aquarius energy is future‑minded, independent, and humanitarian: “How do we make this better for everyone?” Fun bit: star‑watchers once linked the Water Bearer’s season with welcome winter rains—sky myth as weather report.
