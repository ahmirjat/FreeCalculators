Mythology & Stories

Leo is the Nemean Lion of Heracles’ first labor—hide like armor, roar like thunder. The hero outwitted brute force with strategy, then wore the pelt as a sign of earned radiance. Leo energy is solar: creative, regal, unafraid to take the stage and warm the room. Fun bit: ancient festivals timed to the high summer Sun crowned dancers with lion manes—spotlight mandatory.
