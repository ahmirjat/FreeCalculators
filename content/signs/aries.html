Mythology & Stories

Aries is the Ram of the Golden Fleece. In Greek myth, a divine ram rescued Phrixus and Helle, flying them from danger; Helle fell into the sea (the Hellespont), while Phrixus arrived safely and offered the fleece— later hunted by Jason and the Argonauts. Aries energy is head‑first, the moment before “Go!” when the gate opens and the sprint begins. Fun bit: ancient stargazers pictured the Ram charging the sky’s equinox—first on the field, first to claim spring.

Food & Flavors

  • Vibe: bold heat, smoke, and crisp brightness.
  • Herbs & spices: chili, black pepper, garlic, ginger, rosemary, smoked paprika.
  • Fresh notes: arugula, radish, citrus (grapefruit, lemon), pomegranate.
  • Mains: fire‑grilled skewers (veg or meat), spicy stir‑fries, charred street tacos.
  • Drink/Dessert: ginger‑lime soda or espresso tonic; dark chocolate with chili flakes.
