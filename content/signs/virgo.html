♍ Virgo

Evergreen notes • Stories • Food pairings

Mythology & Stories

Virgo is the Maiden, often linked to Astraea, the goddess of justice and purity, who stayed among humans longer than any other deity during the mythic Golden Age. When the world grew harsh, she ascended to the stars, becoming Virgo. This sign’s energy is mindful, practical, and devoted to service—seeing beauty in details. Fun bit: some ancient depictions show her holding wheat, a nod to harvest skills and nourishing care.

Food & Flavors

  • Vibe: clean, balanced, fresh-from-the-garden.
  • Herbs & notes: basil, oregano, parsley, sage, lemon balm.
  • Produce: zucchini, spinach, carrots, apples, grapes.
  • Mains: grain bowls, fresh salads, herb-crusted fish, vegetable soup.
  • Drink/Dessert: green tea or cucumber-mint water; fruit parfait or oat cookies.

Entertaining Bits
