♏ Scorpio

Evergreen notes • Stories • Food pairings

Mythology & Stories

Scorpio is the Scorpion of the night sky, sent (in one tale) to humble Orion after he boasted he could hunt all creatures. The gods placed hunter and scorpion on opposite sides of the heavens so they never meet. Scorpio energy is deep water: transformative, guarded, and fiercely loyal. Fun bit: stargazers joked that when Scorpius rises, Orion “makes an Irish exit.”

Food & Flavors

  • Vibe: dark, spicy, and concentrated—intensity with finesse.
  • Herbs & notes: black garlic, chili, cumin, star anise, cocoa, espresso.
  • Produce: blood orange, beet, blackberry, eggplant, fennel.
  • Mains: smoky mole, peppery noodles, slow‑braised dishes with heat.
  • Drink/Dessert: espresso martini or spiced chai; dark‑chocolate truffles with sea salt.

Entertaining Bits
